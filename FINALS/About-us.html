About us

On Iced Cravings is a cafe that was established in May 2021 and provides meals and caffeinated beverages at affordable and reasonable prices. We use high-quality ingredients and also provide great customer service. The establishment's current owner is Kate Coleen Soriano, and it's current location is at Botchok's Eatery located in Saluysoy 3020 Meycauayan, Philippines.
